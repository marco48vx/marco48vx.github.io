Drift Boss Instructions
Drift Boss is a simple online game where you control a car and try to drift as far as possible without crashing. The game can be played on a computer or on a mobile device such as a tablet or phone. Here are the instructions for playing Drift Boss: On a computer: Visit the Drift Boss website or open the game through an online gaming platform. Press the spacebar to turn left and un-press to turn right. Try to drift as far as possible without hitting any obstacles or driving off the edge of the track. On a mobile device: Open the Drift Boss app on your phone or tablet. Tap the screen to steer left and untap the screen to steer right. Try to drift as far as possible without hitting any obstacles or driving off the edge of the track. Remember to keep an eye on your score and try to beat your high score with each playthrough. Good luck and have fun!
